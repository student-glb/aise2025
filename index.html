About the Conference

Artificial Intelligence (AI) plays a vital role in every area of life now a days. Artificial intelligence not only creates opportunities but also brings greater challenges to the sustainable development of engineering products. AI is helping the next generation of companies to reduce their environmental and social impact by improving efficiency and developing new products.

Therefore, the relationship between AI and sustainable engineering is worth studying. To prioritize sustainable development, AI is a massive technological boost. Sustainability is the greatest challenge in a variety of areas like AI, AR/VR, Robotics, IoT, non-conventional energy and environment, agriculture, health, transportation, etc. It is believed that AI, data analytics and data sciences have significant roles to play in evidence based policy making to support sustainability, as well as providing a tool to monitor and achieve sustainable engineering goals.

AISE 2021 envisions the goal of unifying scientists, researchers and participants from academic as well as industry to present their ideas, innovations and brainstorm to ignite the minds of attendees and peers together in addressing a variety of sustainability challenges across the domains to discuss the challenges and opportunities for sustainable engineering based on AI and supporting tools. The conference will focus on how the AI is useful in achieving the truly sustainable solutions through sharing innovative ideas using transdisciplinary research.

About Yarsi University, Jakarta, Indonesia

YARSI University was founded by the Indonesian Islamic Hospital Foundation (YARSI) initially as the YARSI Medical College on April 15 1967 with one medical study program. The establishment of the YARSI Medical College started out of concern over the scarcity of medical personnel in Indonesia and the very backwardness of Muslims in participating in the field of health services and education. At that time in Jakarta there was not a single medical educational institution or hospital managed by an Islamic institution. Along the way, in 1969, in accordance with the provisions of law number 22 of 1961, this institution was transformed into the YARSI Medical College. With the existence of this educational institution, opportunities are open for prospective students to receive medical education equipped with the Islamic faith which guides people about the nature of life which basically only seeks the pleasure of ALLAH SWT.
